Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kiddora collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you visit or shop on Kiddora, we may collect certain information to provide a secure and personalized shopping experience.

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Account and order information including login credentials and purchase history.
  • Device and usage data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to operate, improve, and secure the Kiddora platform.

  • To process orders, payments, and deliveries.
  • To manage user accounts and provide customer support.
  • To personalize product recommendations and offers.
  • To communicate order updates and important service notifications.
  • To detect fraud and maintain platform security.

3. Sharing of Information

Kiddora does not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only when necessary.

  • With trusted service providers for payments, shipping, and communication.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • In the event of a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

  • Secure servers and encrypted data transmission.
  • Restricted access to sensitive information.
  • Regular monitoring and security updates.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your personal information and how it is used.

  • Access and update your account details at any time.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request account deletion, subject to legal obligations.
  • Manage cookies through your browser settings.

By using Kiddora, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at privacy@kiddora.com.
