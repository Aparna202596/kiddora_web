Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Kiddora uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing and shopping experience.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies help websites remember information about your visit, such as login details, items added to your cart, and browsing preferences, making future visits smoother and more efficient.

1. Data Tracking Using Cookies

Kiddora uses cookies to personalize your experience, understand how our website is used, and improve our services. Cookies allow us to:

  • Remember items in your shopping cart
  • Recognize returning users
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website functionality and performance

These cookies collect non-personally identifiable information such as pages visited and links clicked. They are used strictly for statistical analysis and service improvement.

2. Other Tracking Technologies

We may use technologies such as pixel tags and web beacons to measure website traffic, email engagement, and promotional effectiveness. These technologies do not collect personally identifiable information.

3. Security of Information

Kiddora takes reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Sensitive data is protected using industry-standard security technologies such as SSL encryption. However, no online transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

4. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or cookie usage of those websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

5. Advertising Cookies

Advertising partners may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity. These cookies do not collect personal information such as your name or contact details.

6. Google Analytics & Ads

Kiddora may use Google Analytics and Google Ads cookies to understand user behavior and improve marketing effectiveness. These cookies use anonymous data and do not track personally identifiable information.

7. Children’s Privacy

Kiddora does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18 without parental consent. Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise children’s online activity.

8. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies through their settings. Please note that disabling cookies may limit certain features of the Kiddora website, including checkout and account access.

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at support@kiddora.com
