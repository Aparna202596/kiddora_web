Privacy Policy

Your Trust. Your Privacy. Our Commitment.

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Kiddora, we may collect several types of information to provide you with a seamless shopping experience.

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, delivery address, and payment details collected when you register, place orders, or subscribe to newsletters.
  • Usage Information: Data on pages visited, products viewed, search queries, and purchase history for better service and personalization.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, browser, operating system, and app version to ensure security, compatibility, and performance.
  • Children’s Information: Kiddora caters to kids 0–15 years and does not knowingly collect information from children without parental consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to enhance your experience, process orders, and improve our services:

  • Order Processing & Delivery: Personal and payment information is used to manage orders and deliver products efficiently.
  • Account Management: Manage your Kiddora account securely with email and password information.
  • Personalization: Recommend products, offers, and promotions based on browsing and purchase history.
  • Communication: Send transactional notifications, newsletters, and updates. Promotional communications can be opted out anytime.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detect suspicious activity, prevent unauthorized transactions, and maintain a secure platform.

3. How We Share Your Information

Kiddora does not sell your personal information. We only share data in specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors for order delivery, payment processing, and marketing campaigns.
  • Legal Compliance: Disclosure when required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: Data may be transferred during mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.
  • Parental Sharing: For children’s accounts, some information may be shared with parents or guardians for verification and management purposes.

4. Data Security & Storage

Protecting your data is our priority. Kiddora implements the following measures:

  • Encryption: Sensitive information, including payment details, is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access personal information necessary for their duties.
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with regular updates and monitoring.
  • Retention Policy: We keep data only as long as necessary or required by law and securely delete or anonymize it afterward.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Correction: Update or correct your account information anytime.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications and newsletters.
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal or transactional requirements.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Manage cookies and tracking preferences via browser settings.
  • Contact Us: For privacy inquiries, email our privacy team at privacy@kiddora.com.

At Kiddora, your trust is our priority. By using our website or app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
