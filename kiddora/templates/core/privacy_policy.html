Privacy Policy

Your Trust. Your Privacy. Our Commitment.

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Kiddora, we may collect various types of information to ensure a smooth, secure, and personalized shopping experience for you and your children.

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment details provided during registration or checkout.
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, products viewed, search history, and purchase behavior used to improve recommendations.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and app version to enhance security and performance.
  • Children’s Information: Kiddora serves children aged 0–15 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used responsibly to deliver better services and maintain platform security.

  • Order Processing: To confirm, process, and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Account Management: To create, authenticate, and manage your Kiddora account securely.
  • Personalization: To recommend products, deals, and collections tailored to your preferences.
  • Communication: To send order updates, service notifications, and promotional messages (optional).
  • Fraud Prevention: To monitor suspicious activities and protect against unauthorized access.

3. How We Share Your Information

Kiddora respects your privacy and does not sell or rent your personal information. Data sharing is limited to essential scenarios only.

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners assisting with payments, logistics, and communication.
  • Legal Obligations: Disclosure when required by law or legal proceedings.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers or acquisitions, user data may be transferred securely.
  • Parental Access: Information related to children’s accounts may be shared with parents or guardians.

4. Data Security & Storage

We implement strict technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access.

  • Encryption: Sensitive data such as payment details are encrypted using industry standards.
  • Access Control: Limited access granted only to authorized personnel.
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on protected servers with continuous monitoring.
  • Retention Policy: Information is retained only as long as legally or operationally required.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your personal information and how it is used.

  • Access & Updates: Review and update your account information anytime.
  • Marketing Preferences: Opt out of promotional emails and notifications.
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account, subject to legal requirements.
  • Cookies: Manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Contact: Reach us at privacy@kiddora.com for privacy-related queries.

By using Kiddora, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Your trust is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.
